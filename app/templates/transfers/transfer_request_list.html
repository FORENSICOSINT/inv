How Conversion Factors Work
Core Formula: QTY × CF = Base Units

The system converts all quantities to base units before processing.

Example: Sugar Sachets
  • Base Unit: Sachet (CF = 1)
  • Pack: 1 Pack = 10 sachets
  • Box: 1 Box = 100 sachets
Calculation Examples
  • 3 Boxes: 3 × 100 = 300 sachets
  • 5 Packs: 5 × 10 = 50 sachets
  • 15 Sachets: 15 × 1 = 15 sachets
Why This Matters
  • Single Source of Truth: All inventory is tracked in one base unit
  • Accurate Transfers: Prevents stock mismatches between stores
  • Clean Audits: Easy reconciliation during stock takes
  • Consistent Reporting: All reports use base units for accuracy
Real-World Analogy

Think of money: Base unit = cents. You store $3.50 as 350 cents in the database, not as "3 notes + 5 coins". Same principle applies to inventory.
